Bookmarks Aren't Forever

Remembering a page shouldn't require a permanent fixture in your browser. What you need is a disposable bookmark.
Note Feeder can help.

Tag a bookmark or jot down a note without even leaving your page.
As new bookmarks come in the old ones will fade away guilt free! If you need them again Note Feeder can always find them.
You can even share your bookmark feeds with friends or readers so they can see what you're reading!

Its like delicious without the chatter and pinboard without the fees.
